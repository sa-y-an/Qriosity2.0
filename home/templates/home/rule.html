Rules & Regulations:

  1. Round 1 consists of a total of 10 questions carrying 10 points each .
  2. Solving 1st round shall help to generate a passcode to enter Round 2 following set of Algorithms.
  3. Round 2 carries 5 questions carrying 15 points each .
  4. Questions of Round 1 and Round 2 are supplied by hints ,unlocking which shall further lead to a deduction of 5 points per hint unlocked .
  5. Answers to the questions have to be given in a format that shall not contain any spaces and special characters to separate words and shall all be in lower case . For example to answer for m-rna you must write mrna.
  6. If the question asks for three values ,the answer must be specified in proper sequence of the variables without space.
  7. The one who completes both the round and is able to crack the code shall be the final winner .
  8. In case of a tie ,time taken for final submission shall be the deciding factor .
